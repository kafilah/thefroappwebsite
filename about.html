The Fro App

One day Kafilah googled natural hair games and nothing appeared. On that day she decided to build her own. She knew that she wanted to create an app in which younger girls of color could see themselves in. The Fro App is just the beginning of several creative concepts Kafilah would like to pursue to combine technology with her several interests.
